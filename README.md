# ☕️ Ttara-Pour
> **Open-Source Your Brew.** 
> A community-driven platform for coffee geeks to log, share, and fork pour-over recipes using real-time Bluetooth scale data.

## 📖 About The Project
Brewing coffee is all about controlling physical variables: mass, flow rate, and time. **OpenPour** is designed for home baristas and coffee enthusiasts who want to take their brewing to the next level. 

By integrating with smart Bluetooth scales (like Acaia and Bookoo), this platform allows users to capture their live brewing data, visualize the extraction curve, and share their exact profiles with the community. Found a champion's recipe? "Fork" it, tweak the variables, and make it your own.

## ✨ Key Features
* **Bluetooth Scale Integration:** Seamlessly connect smart scales to track real-time weight, time, and flow rate.
* **Brewing Curve Visualization:** Turn your extraction data into intuitive, beautiful graphs.
* **Recipe Sharing & Forking:** Upload your best brew, or copy and modify ("fork") recipes from other users in the community.
* **Variable Tracking:** Keep a detailed log of your beans, grind size, temperature, and yield.
